WHOOPS! No Internet connection found

The infographic presents the phenomenon of the internet shutdown, analyzing the total outages occurred in 2018. An internet shutdown is a disruption of internet communications caused by technical problems or, more often, a government decision to exert control over some specific events. The visualization aims to show the quantity, the duration, the geographical distribution and the causes of the outages, both declared and suspected. Since governments order the majority of the shutdowns, the visualization presents a deeper level of reading linking the outages to the democracy index of each country. The infographic also displays additional information about some internet disruptions to make the phenomenon clearer. Dowload the visualization
