Making the Invisible Visible

The project explores the online censorship of visual contents that consists of the control or the removal of certain contents that are accessible to the public. The more the phenomenon involves users, the more it obtains relevance, becoming one of the most discussed topics. The research analyzes the topic following different perspectives: the type of censored contents and the languages used to censor them; the people’s reactions to the phenomenon both in terms of offline movement and methods used by the users to avoid it; and the human moderators whose job is censoring online contents. The final result is a website where the users can interact with eight different visualizations aimed to give a comprehensive view of this phenomenon. Website
