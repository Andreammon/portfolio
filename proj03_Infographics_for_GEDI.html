Infographics for GEDI

GEDI Gruppo Editoriale is one of the most important Italian publishers operating in different sectors: newspaper and magazine, radio, internet, and television. GEDI is also the publisher of the newspaper la Repubblica which contains monthly inserts about different topics among which “Salute” which regards arguments related to health, and “Green and Blue” which instead deals with issues related to the environment. These inserts contain dashboards commissioned to Accurat, which I handled during the internship. “Salute” and “Green and Blue” are characterized by a specific colour palette, layout and fonts defined by the client’s graphic team. Therefore the main challenge was to design different visualization always new and understandable keeping them consistent with the visual identity. Website
