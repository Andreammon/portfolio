Esc

Esc is a glocal location-based mobile game meant for university students and based on the novel Petronius, by Marcel Schwob. The main theme of discovery is underlined using the escape as a metaphor that leads the user to explore the university areas, experiencing as a neighbourhood with unique places to discover. The users, that play as the main character, use an application in conjunction with a wrecking journal interacting with meaningful choices that lead to different routes and finals. The narrative is structured in episodes in which most of the protagonist's dialogues and actions that imply a choice or an action by the users are essential for the development of the story. In some places, the users will find artefacts crucial for the continuation of the game. Esc is thought to be adaptable to different university contexts thanks to its flexible narrative structure.
